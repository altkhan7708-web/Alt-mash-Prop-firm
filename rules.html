What is the profit target for the 1-Step and 2-Step Challenges?

Profit targets are configured based on the challenge model selected:

  • 1-Step Challenge: Reaching a profit target of 10% in a single phase.
  • 2-Step Challenge: Reaching 8% in Phase 1 and 5% in Phase 2.
  • Instant Funding: No evaluation target is required. You start with a funded account and receive immediate payout shares.
How do the Daily Drawdown and Maximum overall loss limits work?

Risk limits keep trading disciplined and accounts secure:

  • Daily Loss Limit (Simulated): Calculated based on the start-of-day balance. It is 3% for 1-Step and Instant accounts, and 5% for 2-Step accounts.
  • Maximum Overall Loss (Simulated): This is the total maximum drawdown limit. It is 6% for 1-Step accounts, 10% for 2-Step accounts, and 5% for Instant Funding accounts.

Exceeding any of these limits results in deactivation of your evaluation or funded account.

Is there a minimum number of trading days?

Alt-Mash Trader does not force you to rush your trades, nor wait unnecessarily:

  • 1-Step & Instant Funding: 0 Days. There are no minimum trading days, so you can pass immediately.
  • 2-Step Evaluation: A minimum of 5 Days on both Phase 1 and Phase 2. You must open at least one position on 5 distinct calendar days.
What is the leverage offered during evaluations?

Simulated leverage varies by account structure to help you manage risk properly:

  • Evaluation Challenges (1-Step / 2-Step): Up to 1:100 leverage.
  • Instant Funding: Up to 1:50 leverage.
Can I trade during high-impact news events?

Yes, news trading is fully allowed on all Alt-Mash Trader evaluations and funded accounts. We do not enforce news restriction windows, copy trading blocks during events, or other limitations on news-related strategies.

Am I allowed to hold positions over the weekend?

Yes, weekend holding is permitted on all evaluation phases and funded accounts. Positions do not need to be squared off prior to market close on Friday.

What happens if my account is inactive?

To ensure server efficiency, accounts that register no trading activity for 30 consecutive days will be permanently deactivated. Simply opening or closing a micro-lot position once every 30 days keeps your account active.

Are copy trading and EAs (Expert Advisors) allowed?

We welcome EAs, indicator-based bots, and copy-trading strategies under the following rules:

  • You can use your own EAs or copy trades between your own Alt-Mash accounts.
  • Copy trading between different users or from external third-party signals is prohibited.
  • Arbitrage trading, tick-scalping bots, and latency trading are strictly prohibited.
When can I request my first payout and how often?

On our funded accounts, payouts can be requested **weekly** (every 7 calendar days). Payout processing takes less than 8 hours on average, and you can withdraw directly via Bank Wire, Crypto, or Wise.

How does the 100% Refund policy work?

For the 1-Step and 2-Step challenges, your initial registration fee is 100% refundable. The registration fee amount is paid back in full together with your first successful funded account payout certificate.

How does the Account Scaling plan work?

We reward consistent, profitable traders by scaling accounts:

  • If you achieve a cumulative profit gain of 10% over 4 months, and make at least 2 payouts during this period, we will increase your initial account balance by 25%.
  • This scaling can repeat up to a maximum account size of $1,000,000.
Which trading platforms are supported?

We partner with **cTrader** as our primary platform. cTrader is famous for raw ECN spreads, lightning-fast execution times, deep charting utilities, and a clean interface available on Web, Desktop, iOS, and Android.

How long does it take to receive my account credentials?

All credentials, login keys, and simulated server setup are generated **instantly** after payment confirmation. Setup instructions are emailed directly to your inbox within minutes.

Do you offer support on weekends?

Yes, our customer assistance team is active 24/7. You can reach out via live chat directly on the client dashboard, or by raising a ticket in our official Discord server.
